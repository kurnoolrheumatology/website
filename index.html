About Our Clinic

Welcome to Kurnool Rheumatology Centre

Established with a vision to provide world-class rheumatology care in Kurnool, our clinic offers comprehensive treatment for all rheumatological conditions in a patient-centered environment.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern technology and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals committed to improving the quality of life for our patients.

Located near Amma Hospital in Madhava Nagar, we are easily accessible and dedicated to serving the community with excellence in rheumatology care.

Meet Our Specialist

Dr. Sree Hari Reddy Gadekallu

MD, MRCP - SCE (Rheumatology)

With over 9 years of experience and having treated more than 15,000 patients, Dr. Sree Hari Reddy is a leading rheumatologist committed to providing exceptional care.

Dr. Reddy combines his extensive medical expertise with a compassionate approach, ensuring each patient receives personalized attention and the most effective treatment options available.

His dedication to staying current with the latest advancements in rheumatology enables him to offer cutting-edge care for even the most complex rheumatological conditions.
